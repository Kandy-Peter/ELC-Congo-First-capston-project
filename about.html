ELC-Congo online open class 2021

ELC-Congo(E-learning classroom in DR. Congo) was founded in january 2020 to help young people have access to any level of education especially those who are most deprived of it. Our community provides scholarship and many ways to partcipate in the biggest challenges or even get your dream job. We are here to help you to become who you want to be.
